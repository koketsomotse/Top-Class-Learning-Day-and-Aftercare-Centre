Our Exceptional School Facilities

At Top Class Learning Day and Aftercare Centre, we provide a nurturing and stimulating environment where children thrive. Our facilities are designed to foster learning, play, and development, ensuring a well-rounded experience for every child. We are committed to providing:

  • Exciting Extras: Swimming lessons to build confidence and coordination in a fun and safe setting.
  • Qualified Team: Our staff includes a Bachelor of Education student and graduate, bringing expertise and passion to your child’s care.
  • Healthy Nutrition: We provide 2 wholesome meals and a snack each day to keep your little one energized.
  • Convenient Hours: Open from 06:30 to 17:00 to fit your family’s busy schedule.
